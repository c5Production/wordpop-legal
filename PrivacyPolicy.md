# Privacy Policy for WordPop

**Last updated:** March 12, 2025

**Effective date:** March 12, 2025

WordPop ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our word puzzle game.

---

## Information We Collect

**WordPop collects minimal information:**

- **Game statistics (stored on your device only):** Score, games played, words submitted, streaks, and similar gameplay data. This information is stored locally on your device and is **not** sent to our servers or any third party.

- **No personal information:** We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, or any other personally identifiable information.

- **No account required:** You can play WordPop without creating an account or providing any personal details.

---

## How We Use Information

- Game statistics stored on your device are used **only** to display your progress, streaks, and achievements within the app.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

---

## Children's Privacy

WordPop is designed for users ages 4 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we do not collect personal information, we are compliant with children's privacy regulations (e.g., COPPA) to the extent applicable.

---

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on your device using your device's standard storage mechanisms.
- We do not transmit your data to external servers.
- You may delete all app data by uninstalling WordPop from your device.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, where possible, through an in-app notice or App Store update description.
